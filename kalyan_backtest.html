The Fundamental Math
Payout: Bet ₹1 → Win ₹9 (net +₹8) | Lose → net -₹1
Base hit chance: 1 in 10 = 10.0% per pick per draw
Break-even win rate: Need 11.11% hits to cover cost
Your edge: Model achieves ~10.7–11.3% hit rate (marginal +)
Expected Value per Bet (1 pick, each side):
EV = P(hit) × ₹9 − P(miss) × ₹1
EV = 0.107 × 9 − 0.893 × 1 = ₹ −0.07 per ₹1 bet
House edge: ~7% — this game is designed so the house wins long-term
⚠ KEY FINDING
All pick counts lose money over 3000+ days. The fewer picks = smaller losses.
1 pick loses ~₹234 on ₹1000. 5 picks loses ~₹2808.
The scoring model gives a ~1.1–1.3% edge over random — not enough to overcome the 9x payout house edge.
✓ The model DOES have real predictive value — just not enough to beat the payout structure.
